ABOUT US

Our Story

Crafting Comfort & Confidence in Every Home

Founded in April 2020 by Joseph Oladiran, Joruthe LLC began as a small, passionate endeavor to bring quality and reliability into the home repair and renovation industry. With a background in building technology and civil engineering, Joseph saw an opportunity to blend technical expertise with a personal touch, transforming houses into homes that not only look beautiful but feel right.

Over the years, we've grown from a one-person operation into a thriving team of skilled professionals, each of us dedicated to making your home renovation dreams come true. Our journey has been built on a foundation of trust, craftsmanship, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs.

We believe in not just meeting expectations but exceeding them, ensuring every project, big or small, is completed with the utmost care and precision. At Joruthe LLC, we're more than just builders, we're creators of comfort and confidence, transforming spaces into personalized sanctuaries where life's best moments happen.



Meet the Team

Meet the Experts Behind Your Home Transformations

Our team is the heart of Joruthe LLC. Each member brings a unique set of skills and a shared passion for making your home the best it can be. Get to know the experts dedicated to turning your projects into successes.

Joseph Oladiran - CEO, brings over 40 years of experience, specialized knowledge, and a personal touch to his role. Born in Nigeria, he has overseen numerous projects, from simple family homes to large-scale reticulatory water systems and reservoirs. An adept chess player, Joseph loves to see a well-thought-out project plan executed to the finest detail, reflecting his strategic and meticulous approach.

Carlos Melendez - Lead Carpenter. Carlos Melendez brings over 20 years of experience as a lead carpenter. Originally from Hounduras, he takes pride in his meticulous work and passion for crafting beautiful spaces.
Carlos specializes in custom cabinetry, millwork, and finish carpentry. When not working , Carlos enjoys spending time with his family and playing soccer.


Senjour Marley - Master Plumber. Senjour Marley brings over 15 years of experience as a licensed master plumber.
Originally from Haiti, he takes pride in his meticulous work and passion for problem-solving. When not working, Senjour enjoys woodworking and music.

Joy Tjesnimi - Interior Designer. With over 10 years of experience in interior design, Joy has a keen eye for transforming spaces into stylish and functional areas.
Specializing in modern and sustainable designs, she believes that a well-designed home can improve daily life and bring joy and satisfaction.
Outside of work, Joy enjoys exploring flea markets for unique finds and is an avid DIY enthusiast.

Charles Parker, with over 15 years of experience as a lead in garage door specialization, epitomizes the blend of precision and rhythm in both his professional and personal life. Charles's expertise spans installation, repairs, and refurbishing of garage doors, ensuring each project is handled with the utmost care and technical skill. Beyond his professional achievements,
Charles, originally from Indiana, is a passionate musician, leading his private band as the drummer. His dual dedication to craftsmanship in garage door services and music showcases a unique balance of discipline and creativity, making him a distinguished figure in his field.

Dave Gudmann is not just a Master Electrician; he's a testament to where skill, passion, and inspiration can lead you in life. Born and raised in the hearty Rust Belt, where resilience and hard work are a way of life,
Dave brings more than just technical expertise to his craft. He's a man who dreams in circuits and wires, often saying that some of his best work was first visualized amid the surreal landscapes of his dreams.


Outside the complexity of electrical systems, Dave is an avid marksman and hunter, finding solace and balance in the quiet of nature. His love for hunting is matched only by his devotion as a family man, where he channels the same precision and focus into nurturing and protecting his loved ones.


Watching Dave work is akin to observing a seasoned artist at the canvas; every move is deliberate, every decision backed by years of experience and a deep understanding of his craft. His tales of drawing inspiration from the wiring schematics of his dreams add a layer of mystique to his already fascinating persona, making him not just a master of his trade but a storyteller whose narratives are as compelling as the electrical systems he brings to life.
